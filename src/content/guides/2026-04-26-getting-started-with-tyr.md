Welcome to Tyr. This guide covers the core ideas every new player should internalize in their first few hours.

## Pick a chassis you can stomach losing

Early matches are about learning lines of sight, not winning. Pick a vehicle you don't mind dying in.

## Map awareness beats raw firepower

Vision and detection control most engagements. Glance at the minimap **before** you reposition, not after.

## Build slowly

Don't lock in a build until you've played the chassis a few times. Components are easier to swap once you know what's actually hurting you in matches.

Welcome aboard — and good hunting.
